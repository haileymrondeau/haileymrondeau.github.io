Funding

An IndieGoGo campaign will be held to raise funds for the film, with various funding perks available. These perks will include:

  • A digital copy of the film
  • A physical copy of the film on DVD
  • Special thanks in the credit
  • Shout-out on social media
  • Associate Producer credits*
  • Co-Producer credits*
  • Executive Producer credits*

*These roles grant no creative control over the film

Marketing

Social media accounts on platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and X (formerly Twitter) will be created to promote the film during the production phase. These accounts will feature shout-outs to project backers, behind the scenes looks at the production process, and updates on the release date, showings, and festival entries for the film.

Distribution

The film will premiere as part of the Maine Mayhem film festival in May of 2026. Alongside the Maine Mayhem showings, the film will be entered into various festivals, and the film will be available in both digital and physical formats as a fundraising perk.
