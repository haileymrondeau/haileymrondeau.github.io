If I could pick any universe to live in, it would be the ones behind my screen.


I grew up inside cinematic universes created by my favorite YouTubers from nothing but video games and post-production. Those videos made me realize: I don’t just want to live in a different universe, I want to be the one to create it. I thrive off of helping aspiring filmmakers bring their visions to life. To me, every frame is an important piece of the puzzle, and I can't rest until it reaches perfection. Nothing is more magical to me than bringing a film to life with my own two hands.
